ABOUT

HI, I'M DARIA.

I started my journey in a technical field, graduating in Mechanical Engineering, but quickly realized I was drawn to creating rather than just building systems. That curiosity led me into the world of 3D, where I found the perfect balance between logic and creativity.

NOW I WORK AS A 3D ARTIST, FOCUSING ON 3D MOTION DESIGN, VISUALIZATION AND FOOTWEAR DEVELOPMENT.

I’m deeply interested in everything related to 3D — from modeling and animation to design development — and I also explore web development. This website, by the way, is something I designed and built myself.

I’m constantly learning, exploring new tools and approaches, and improving my skills in the areas I already work in. Growth is a big part of who I am as a creator. I love turning ideas into clean, visually strong, and practical results — whether it’s a complex system or a small detail.
