Marakova Daria 3d artist

For each project I choose the workflow and tools that best suit its specific creative and technical requirements. I do not limit myself to a single production pipeline, as I believe that different tasks require different approaches.

I create clothing and fabric simulations in CLO 3D, while Blender is my primary tool for 3D modeling, animation, lighting and rendering. For projects that require more organic and detailed forms, I also work with ZBrush. For complex particle work or lighting to create realistic bakery products, I use Houdini and Blender depending on the task.

When a project involves fluid simulations, particle systems, procedural animation or other computationally complex effects, I usually work in Houdini. I enjoy its flexibility and the level of control it provides when building simulations and procedural systems.

For texturing, I primarily use Substance 3D Painter. However, when a project does not require baked texture maps or a complex texturing pipeline, I may create materials directly in Blender or in the software used for the final render, depending on the project requirements. I have also worked in Cinema 4D with Arnold and Redshift.

For camera and object tracking, I have experience with SynthEyes, but I also enjoy experimenting with alternative solutions. For one of my projects, I created a facial tracking setup directly in Blender, which allowed me to explore a more customized approach to the task.

I use After Effects and DaVinci Resolve for compositing, editing, colour correction and final post-production.

My workflow is flexible and constantly evolving. For every project, I try to find the most efficient and visually effective solution rather than following the same process each time. Whenever the schedule allows, I also use projects as an opportunity to experiment with new software, techniques and production methods. This approach helps me expand my technical skills while continuing to develop my own visual style.

CGI for CSB

An independently produced commercial CGI series developed from my original concepts. I created all assets and handled modelling, look development, animation, lighting and Cycles rendering in Blender, using Houdini for the bag's fabric simulation. I also completed the editing and sound design.

CGI video for Demix

Created in-house for Demix, with the complete creative and production process handled solely by me. Depending on the project, I used CLO 3D, Houdini and Blender for footwear creation, procedural effects, animation and rendering, followed by final editing and sound design.

CGI for FORM

A self-directed commercial fashion project based on my own concept and storyboard. I created and animated the characters in Blender, designed and simulated their garments in CLO 3D, rendered the final scenes in Cycles, and completed the post-production and sound design.

Meshki

An ongoing independent commercial collaboration for which I develop each film from the initial idea to final delivery. My work includes concept development, visual storytelling, modelling, garment simulation in CLO 3D, animation and rendering in Blender, as well as editing and sound design.

The Runaway Label

A fully self-produced commercial fashion CGI project combining surreal environments with animated garments and accessories. I created the concept and all visual assets, built and simulated the clothing and bags in CLO 3D, assembled and rendered the scenes in Blender Cycles, and handled the final edit and sound design.

Personal Project

A self-initiated project created primarily to explore new software workflows and use render passes for the first time. I created the character and animated every movement by hand in Blender, then rendered the scene in separate passes for compositing in Nuke. The project allowed me to experiment with greater control in post-production, including masks, motion blur and individual pass adjustments.

Bushe Bakery

A series of commercial CGI videos developed around original characters and product-led storytelling. I designed, modelled, rigged and animated the characters in Blender, created the environments and developed the actions for each scene to communicate the story and personality of the featured product.
